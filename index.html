India Intelligence
Not a Generic Tool. Built for Indian Substations.

Most earthing tools are built for Western conditions. Indian substations are different. Earthing Expert knows the difference.

Black Cotton Soil
High seasonal resistivity swings, aggressive corrosion. Automatically flags Vidarbha, Marathwada, central India sites.
Coastal / Saline
Chloride-driven galvanic corrosion of GI. Recommends copper and alerts to accelerated degradation risk.
Rocky Laterite
High surface resistivity — step voltage governs. Recommends counterpoise and surface layer treatment.
GI Theft Risk
Notes where copper justifies its premium — especially at critical HV equipment connection points.
Chemical Earthing
Acceptable as supplement only — never a replacement for an IS 3043 designed grid. Always clarifies this.
CEIG Inspection
Reminds you of required documents — IS 3043 compliance, measured earth resistance records, as-built drawings.
